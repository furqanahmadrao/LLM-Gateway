Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
Version 2.1, January 2018

Our Pledge
----------
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors
and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards
---------
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable Behavior
---------------------
Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Public or private harassment of any contributor
- Publishing others' private information without their consent
- Other conduct which a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

Enforcement
-----------
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by contacting the project team at furqanrao091@gmail.com. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that
is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is
obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an
incident.

Scope
-----
This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces
when an individual is representing the project or its community.
